Our Mission

Main Gates is a musical Group under the Seventh-day-adventist church based in the western province of Zambia, Kaoma District. This group was established in the year 2014 at Kaoma Main Church, and since then it has envolved into becoming one of the most powerful singing groups under the SDA church with serious emphasis on taking the gospel of Christ to all the world through songs. Main gates is more than just the members of the singing group that actively take part in music but it extends its membership to even other believers who would want to take part in the vision that the group has, as such serious members with the passion for the gospel are accommodated and called Main Gates Fellows. This proves a point that Main Gates is more than just preaching the gospel through music but also involves its self in many other evangelistic activities under the Seventh-day-adventist church.

The Team

A team of Gallant men ready to work for their master at all times through music and other community based work. Apart from music we also venture into other gospel programs like community outreach and musical fellowships.

We have alot of other members from different parts of the country that we call Main Gates Fellows that help in organising the many events that we undertake as a group.
