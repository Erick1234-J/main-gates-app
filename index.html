Our Motto

We the Main Gates are a musical group under the Seventh-Day Adventist Church with a Motto of drawing souls to christ through music. We are an independent group with members all around zambia but committed to one purpose which is spreading the Gospel through songs as we await for the coming of the jesus christ.

Read More

The Team

A team of Gallant men ready to work for their master at all times through music and other community based work. Apart from music we also venture into other gospel programs like community outreach and musical fellowships.

We have alot of other members from different parts of the country that we call Main Gates Fellows that help in organising the many events that we undertake as a group.

Services Info

Seventh-Day Adventist

Service To God and Man

We strive to foster the work of God through our spirit filled uplifting music.

Gospel Trigger

We have a campaign we are calling Gosple Trigger which is just a challenge to a believer to remember their first love for God and begin to take the work of God seriously.
